About Newport

Take a look into the different activities you can endure in the beautiful place of Newport, Rhode Island. Check out the history, restaurants, tours, ocean, and waterfront specialities. You will never go bored in a place like this.

  • Fresh and delicious seafood!
  • Everywhere you go there will be a view
  • Check out the history and artifacts that remain in Newport
  • Walk along the shops that stretch through the town consisting of boutiques and architectural buildings

Beaches

Come to Newport and catch some sun at these gorgeous beaches. A fresh tan will look amazing while walking around Newport!

Don't forget your sunscreen! Go-To: Easton's Beach, Second Beach, or Gooseberry Beach. Narragansett Town Beach is about 30 minutes from Newport (it is my favorite beach in the world)!

Grab a Bite & Drink

There is an abundance of restaurants along the water where you could grab a variety of foods.

Personally I am a huge seafood person, especially in pasta!

I Recommend: The Mooring, The Red Parrot, The Black Pearl, The Landing, The Lobster Bar, or 22 Bowens! Check out the photos to see the scenery and food at The Landing!

Boat Tour

Visit Tripadvisor before coming to Newport and book a sunset or day tour on the water!

Afternoon Grand Tour | Gansett Cruises, is my absolute favorite. You get taken around the entire shore and marina of Newport and the tour guide is really cool! Plus they will offer you alcoholic beverages, water, or Del's Lemonade!

Where to Shop

Obviously when you are in Newport, you need to walk around all of the shops in order to get the full experience.

Head to the following stores to find some flavored popcorn, jewerly, clothing, sweats, or hot sauce!

The Salty Babe, Inpopnito Popcorn, Life is Good Newport, Newport Original, and any other shops in the vicinity of those!
